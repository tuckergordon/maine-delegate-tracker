How this is counted. Attribution is inferred: county results list who was elected but not whom they support, so each winner is matched by name to the campaigns' published delegate slates. Someone on exactly one slate is credited to that candidate; on two or more they are counted as Ambiguous (never credited to anyone); on no slate as Unmatched (e.g. automatic/ex-officio delegates). Alternates are excluded.

Campaign slates change over time and are re-checked each update. Not an official pledge count. Totals cover only counties that have reported.
